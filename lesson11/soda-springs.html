Spring is Around the Corner

by Carri Jameson

I'm sure we can all agree that this last winter storm has got all of us ready and rearing for spring to finally come. If this applies to you, then I've got some great news: Spring is on its way! According to local forecasts the coming weeks should bring some incremental rising of the temperatures. We should see most of the snow melting in the town area and some tulips and daffodils peaking out of the dirt in the gardens around the Town Hall buidling. We're not fully in the clear yet. There will probably be a mild snow storm or two before we hit mid April, but have hope! Now is the time to start planting seedlings indoors so they'll be ready to move outside come garden season.
