Lazy Summer Day Activities

by Susan Durfey

It's getting to be that time of year that we're all looking forward to, when the days are getting warmer and longer. School is almost over and our time becomes more our own. So, the question remains, how do we keep ourselves and our children entertained? This can be especially hard in small towns like Fish Haven. Thankfully, we have a beautiful lake and tons of outdoor recreation close by to help us find some entertainment. We also have many more amazing things to see and do! Minetonka caves are close by and, even in the hotter summer months, are a nice cool place to visit. There's Glacier Lake that's fed by melting glaciers all summer long.
