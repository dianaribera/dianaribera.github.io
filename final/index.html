Commerce Purpose:

By combining networking opportunities for businesses of all sizes with a robust Legislative Affairs presence in Utah, the Chamber is better able to meet its mission of advancing business prosperity in Kaysville. By utilizing the strengths of its general membership, the Chamber’s influence is felt throughout our county and state.

Goals:

  • Provide growth opportunities for local business
  • Create a stronger community
  • Represent the needs and voices of business owners in our community
  • Bring recognition to small businesses
