Primary

This color will be used for the header and other large blocks of color on the site. It will also be used a heading colors when on a white background.

The secondary color will be used as background colors and possibly link colors.

Both accent colors will be used sparingly and only in places that need to stand out - namely call to actions, or a hover state on buttons, etc.
