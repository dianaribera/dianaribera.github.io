Target Audience

There are two main audiences that use the site. The first group are mainly males between the ages of 20 and 35 who want to check weather and events going on in the local community. They check the site before making plans for their weekend adventures. The second group are moms with active children. They want to know what the weather will be like for their childrens sports activities and see what going on in the community so they can attend.

Scenarios

  1. I have family coming to visit. Is there anything fun happening that we can go do?
  2. Is it going to rain while I'm camping this weekend?
  3. Where are the best firework shows going to be on the 4th of July?
  4. My kids have a game tomorrow night, what's the weather supposed to be like?
  5. We missed the homecoming game, I wonder what the score was?
